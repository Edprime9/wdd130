Morrison Rafters

At Morrison Rafters, we are passionate about delivering the ultimate whitewater rafting experiences. Our mission is to inspire a love for adventure while prioritizing safety, sustainability, and unforgettable memories.

History

Founded in the heart of Rivers State, Nigeria, Morrison Rafters started as a dream to share the beauty and thrill of rafting with the world. With over a decade of experience, we have grown into a trusted name in adventure tourism, known for our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our team is composed of skilled guides and support staff who are as passionate about rafting as they are about making your experience extraordinary. From navigating challenging rapids to sharing local knowledge, our crew ensures your trip is as safe as it is exhilarating.
